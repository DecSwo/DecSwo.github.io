Declan Swoyer


Declan Patrick Swoyer (born April 15, 2004) is a full-time student, athlete, and part-time retail service advocate. He is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree from The Pennsylvania State University in the IT field. He is majoring in Information Sciences & Technology with a Cybersecurity focus and is minoring in Security & Risk Analysis. Outside his schooling, he is passionate about physical fitness, eating healthy, and maintaining positive mental health so that he can boost the same for others. He has played soccer for all of his life, eventually playing for both PIAA and club teams throughout middle and high school. He has also played volleyball and basketball, though he has been most passionate about weightlifting and powerlifting since 2023.

Early Life

Swoyer was born in Reading, Pennsylvania to parents of predominantly western-european origin. His mother, Bridget Heckman (1981-), is an elementary and middle school mathematics teacher at Oley Valley School District. His father, Travis Swoyer (1979-), is a chef and employee at Berks Career and Technology Center. Declan grew up in Mt. Penn, a small borough in Berks County, PA. He attended Antietam School District for K-12 schooling, where he was involved in extracurricular activities and athletics. He has always been passionate about video games as his favorite entertainment source, with The Legend of Zelda series being his favorite of all time. The Playstation 2-4 and Nintendo Wii & DS were his favorite gaming consoles, up until he built his own desktop PC in 2019 and has primarily gamed on it since. He graduated from Antietam Middle Senior High School as his class's Salutatorian in 2022, attending Penn State Berks in the following fall.

Career

General contractor

Swoyer started his first official job as a home renovation/general contractor for Kehl Haus Inc., a local Berks County business. He learned extensive hands-on skills in the home renovation field, learned time management skills, and was able to speak with clients about their specific needs in order to tailor their experience towards the best direction possible.

Sales associate

Swoyer currently works part-time for the Target Corporation as a front-of-store sales associate. His primary duties include delivering Drive-Up orders and fulfilling guest service needs; though his secondary duties include cashiering, collecting carts, and collecting trash when needed. He has learned extensive social interaction and especially time management skills. He has been with the company for 1.5 years.
